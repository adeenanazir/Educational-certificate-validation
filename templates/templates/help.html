Welcome to the Certificate Validation System Help page. Here you will find information on how to use the system, troubleshoot common issues, and get in touch with our support team.

Getting Started

To get started with validating a certificate, follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the Home page.
  • Click on the "Get Started" button.
  • login to see dashboard
  • click on upload to upload your documents
  • Upload your certificate file and Click "Submit" to validate your certificate.
  • Click "Submit" to validate your certificate.

Common Issues

Issue: Unable to Upload Certificate

If you encounter an issue uploading your certificate, please ensure the file format is supported. Currently, we support PDF and image formats (JPG, PNG).

Issue: Certificate Not Valid

If your certificate is marked as invalid, please verify the following:

  • Make sure that you submitted your exact candidate id
  • The certificate was issued by a recognized institution.
  • The certificate has not expired.
  • All information on the certificate is accurate and unaltered.
